About us

Jagran Institute of Management powered by Knowledge and Steered by Ethical values with a "Global Competencies and Indian Roots" is an educational initiative of Dainik Jagran, a group well known in the corporate and mediator preserving professional and ethical values. Jagran Institute of Management is the most modern, purpose built business school in Uttar Pradesh. As well as being a place of study the building is a place of work in a very modern sense. There are dozens of rooms available for project work, brainstorming and simply getting on with business. Well developed lush green campus with sports facilities.
